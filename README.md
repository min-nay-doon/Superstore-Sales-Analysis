# Superstore-Sales-Analysis
Full Stacks Project (Python+SQL+Power BI)
# Project Overview
This project is end-to-end data analytics solution built suing Python, SQL, and Power BI. It analyzes retail sales data to uncover key business insights related to sales performance, profitabilit, and customer behavior.
# Objectives
- Analyze overall sales and profit performance
- Identify high-perfoening and loss-making products
- Undrestand customer purchasing behavior
- Explore trends over time and seadonal patterns
- Built an interactive dashboard for business decision-making
# Dataset
- Source: Superstore dataset
- Contains information on orders, customers, products, sales, profit and shipping details
- Data includes multiple years, ptoduct categories, and customer segment
# Tools & Techniques
- Python(Data cleaning and transformation)

- Excel (initial data exploration)
# Data Preparation

# Key Insights
- Technology category generates the highest revenue around $836K
- Sales peak during year-end (Nov-Dec), indicating strong seasonality
- Tables, bookcases and supplies are major loss-making sub-categories
- A small group of customers contributes significantly to total revenue
- Consumer segment accounts for the largest share of sales (50%)
- Not all high-revenue products and customers are profitable
# Conclusion
The analysis highlights revenue growth with profitability. While certain categories and customers drive high sales, inefficiencies in pricing and cost management lead to losses in specific areas. By optimizing product strategies, discount policies, and customer targeting, businesses can significantly improve overall performance.
